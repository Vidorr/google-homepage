Cereal

Description

Cereals are a staple breakfast dish around the world. With this simple recipe we will learn how to prepare a quick and easy breakfast that the whole family can enjoy.

Ingredients

  • Boxed or bagged cereal
  • Milk (dairy, almond or soy)

Steps

  1. Pour the disired amount of your chosen cereal into a bowl.
  2. Add milk to bowl till it is slightly below the level of the cereal.
  3. Relax and Enjoy
