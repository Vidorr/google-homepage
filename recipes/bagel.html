Bagel

Description

Bagels are a staple breakfast dish around the world. With this simple recipe we will learn how to prepare a quick and easy breakfast that the whole family can enjoy.

Ingredients

  • Plain Bagel
  • Cream Cheese

Steps

  1. Slice bagel in halves. (or separate if pre-sliced)
  2. Place bagel halves in toaster on low setting.
  3. Once toasting is complete, spread a thin layer of cream cheese on the inside surface of each half.
  4. Optionally, top with additional toppings, such as fresh fruit slices or Everything seasoning.
